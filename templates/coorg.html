Explore Coorg


Coorg is a hill station in the Karnataka State of India. It is very famous for its tea and coffee plantations in India..



Abbey Falls

About 9 km from Madikeri is the beautiful sight of an impetuous mountain stream cascading over a rockface.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple's entrance is adorned with massive multicoloured paintings on its exterior walls.

Bhagamandala

Bhagamandala is a religious place located 39 km from Madikeri.
