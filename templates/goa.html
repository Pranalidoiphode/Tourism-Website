Explore Goa


Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India within the Konkan region
Goa can be the perfect destination for a short holiday.



Calangute Beach

Calangute Beach is the longest beach in North Goa, stretching from Candolim to Baga.

Aguada Fort

Fort Aguada is a 17th-century Portuguese fort looking out at the confluence of Mandovi River and the Arabian Sea.

Church

The Our Lady of Immaculate Conception Church is a very old gem in the list of churches in Goa.
