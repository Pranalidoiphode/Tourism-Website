Explore Rajasthan


Rajasthan is the largest state of the Republic of India in terms of area.
Famous historian James Tad named this state as Raithan or Rajasthan in "Annals and Antiquities of Rajasthan"



Jaisalmer Fort

The city's most prominent landmark is the Jaisalmer Fort, also called Sonar Qila(Golden Fort).

Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh has been the headquarters of the senior branch of Rajput clan known as the Rathores

Amber Fort

The fort is strategically situated atop a hill, providing a vantage point.
